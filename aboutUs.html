About Us

S.O.S.H.A.P. stands for Stop Overthinking Spread Happiness And Positivity.
Nowadays, almost half of the population is dealing with depression, stress and anger issues.
One day , sitting on the chair having my green tea, I was studying psychology and psychodynamic
psychotherapy. Then this idea came in my mind. Basically, the idea behind this is to spread happiness
and positive vibes. Some people feel difficult to express their emotions while others are extroverts.
People face difficulties due to over thinking and stressful life. I am trying to help those people.
There is also page named “Soshap” on Instagram which is always open for the ones who want any
psychological help. I am always available to talk , listen and help you. It is small initiative to make this world
free of depression and anxiety.
