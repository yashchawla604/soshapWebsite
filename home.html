What is overthinking?

Thinking about a person, thing, place, topic, etc. over and over again is referred as overthinking. Stressing out with the thoughts running in our mind has always been dangerous to our health. Individual dealing with some family problems or other life problems like job, girlfriend, exams, etc. leads to overthinking and making themselves a victim of dangerous mental disease, what we call depression in other words. In this, one will not be able to stop thinking. Random thoughts keep haunting the person in regular intervals. It is a like a never ending chain of thoughts. It takes not much time to covert a happy calm person into an angry ,mad headed one.

How to avoid thinking too much?

  1. Perform once decided

  2. Divert mind in different directions

  3. Meditation

  4. Stop future predictions

  5. Believe in yourself
