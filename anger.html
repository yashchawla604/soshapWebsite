Anger

Never argue with someone when you are angry. You can hurt a person to such an extent sometimes
that things cannot be normal after that. Avoid to arguing in anger because whenever you are angry,
you feel that everyone on this planet is wrong except you. You don’t try to understand or listen to anyone.
Which mostly annoys the other person and that can be devastating.

Try not to eat when you are angry or pissed off. Reason is that you will eat more than usual and also mostly
junk. Because if noticed, whenever you feel angry, you want food to neutralize it. And most of the time people eat
pizzas, pasta, chicken and a lot of junk which is not good for their health and they don’t realize at that time. But
regret at the end, resulting in more anger issues.
